This project focused on building a multi-functional smart street light system based on an IoT perception network. It combined lighting control, environmental sensing, traffic monitoring, and data visualisation to support smart city management.

My work involved assisting with data collection, system-function analysis, and the organisation of technical materials for the project demonstration.
