## Goal

Design a multi-functional smart street light system that integrates environmental sensing, traffic monitoring, lighting control, and data visualisation for smart-city scenarios.

## My Contribution

As project lead, I managed the project structure, coordinated functional design, organised sensing and application scenarios, and prepared the technical presentation materials.

## Outcome

The project demonstrated how IoT perception networks can transform traditional street lights into intelligent urban nodes for monitoring, data collection, and city management.
